Assignment 1: Bauhaus ​


Paul Klee's Naval Observatory (Seewarte) from Harvard Art Museums' collection.

Inspired by Paul Klee's painting, my creation is an reimagined work telling the story of what-could-be before and during the eclipse.
Actions for the user include: ​

• mouseX, mouseY – The sun (and eclipse moons) follows the mouse cursor.
• mouseIsPressed – The eclipse occurs when the mouse button is held down.
• LEFT_ARROW, RIGHT_ARROW - Control the protagonist of the story. ​
